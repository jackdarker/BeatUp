BeatUp – Beat'm Up Game Template



Design-Considerations

  • use only 4 direction-keys and A,B,X,Y to be able to play with joystick or keyboard

Combat-Mechanics

  • A and X are used for attack moves while B and Y are used for blocks

  • The pawn has a lower and upper body damage zone. The back side is even more sensitive to damage

About Stamina

  • without stamina the damage of the moves degrade and the pawn is vulnerable

  • Moves/blocks consume stamina

  • stamina regenerates over time

  • Counter-attacks restore stamina immediatly

Basic-Moves

Note that in fight-mode the character is always facing in one direction.

In Fight-mode the following moves are possible:

  • Punch (X):

    • forward attack with fist against upper body

  • Upper-Cut (Up+X):

    • forward attack with fist against upper body

  • High-Kick (Right +X):

    • forward attack with leg against upper body

  • Low-Kick (Right +X):

    • forward attack with leg against lower body

  • Jump-Kick (Up +A):

    • forward attack with leg against lower body.throws the attacked into air

  • Leg-Sweeper(Down +A):

    • forward attack with leg against lower body; throws the attacked to ground

  • Block (B blocks low attacks / Y blocks high attacks)

    • Blocks attacks in one damage zone in forward direction.

    • If the block is initiated shortly before the attack, it is a TimedBlock.

  • Counter (X / A)

    • A TimedBlock can lead to a counter by pressing the corresponding attack-button.

  • Throw ():

    • Counter-Attack;

  • Grab ():

    • Counter-Attack;

  • Dash (Right, Right +B):

    • forward dash attack against lower body

  • Turn around( Left, Left):

    • changes the forward facing direction

Combo-System

  • basic moves can be chained together to create combos

  • the chain can be interupted by blocks
